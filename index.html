Welcome to On The Water

We offer day and half-day boat trips along the Athenian Riviera.

At On The Water, we offer unforgettable boat rental experiences in the crystal-clear waters of Porto Rafti, Greece. Whether you're looking for a relaxing day at sea, a private cruise with friends and family, or an adventurous escape exploring hidden coves, our Cranchi Perla 25 is the perfect choice.

With a capacity of up to 6 guests, our luxury boat ensures comfort, safety, and a personalized experience tailored to your needs. Enjoy sunbathing on deck, snorkeling in turquoise waters, or simply cruising along the stunning coastline with breathtaking views.

Book your trip today and embark on a journey filled with relaxation, adventure, and the beauty of the Aegean Sea!
